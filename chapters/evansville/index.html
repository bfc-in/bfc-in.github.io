Evansville

Evansville

Steve Edwards ( Duke ) is the Evansville Chapter Elder.

Chapter Links Facebook Chapter Elder 812-431-9378  ( home ) duke@bfc-in.org Member Profile

The Evansville chapter is a group of Christian bikers that believe in the sanctity of fellowship and doing things together as a group.

Our chapter meets weekly for fellowship and monthly for our chapter meeting.

We attend bike nights, bike rallies, and other biker events as a group. On occasion, we mingle at car shows. We endeavor to participate in the BFC State, Regional, and National Rallies.

We are always looking for opportunities to spread the Truth in our Jerusalem.
