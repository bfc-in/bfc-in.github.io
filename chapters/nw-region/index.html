NW Region

John Hussell ( Grey-Wolf ) is the NW Region Chapter Elder.

Chapter Links Facebook Chapter Elder 304-812-3111  ( cell ) oldyellow1@msn.com Member Profile

Bikers For Christ Indiana Northwest Region (NW Region) are regular attendees of many different motorcycle events in Indiana. We are a group of Believers in Christ Jesus that are both missionaries and servants unto our Lord Jesus Christ.

You can find us shining boots at a local Harley-Davidson shop, or praying for a brother on the side of the road in need. We are involved in prison and street ministry. We support other clubs benefit rides and charity events. We meet regularly for Bible study, and fellowship to strengthen our relationships and ministry.

Check us out we are true brothers and sisters in our Lord Jesus Christ.
